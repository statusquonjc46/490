Welcome to Automotive Recall Service Center. Here you can check if there is any open recall on your car. Also, its a great way to buy used car because you can check if the car's recall has been fixed or not. A recall is issued when a manufacturer or NHTSA determines that a vehicle, equipment, car seat, or tire creates an unreasonable safety risk or fails to meet minimum safety standards. Most decisions to conduct a recall and remedy a safety defect are made voluntarily by manufacturers prior to any involvement by NHTSA.Manufacturers are required to fix the problem by repairing it, replacing it, offering a refund.
